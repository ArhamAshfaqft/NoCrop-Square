Installation Guide

Quick guide to getting OptiSnap running on your machine.

Step 1

Download & Install

Once you purchase the Lifetime Deal, download the installer for your OS (Windows .exe or macOS .dmg).

Step 2 (Windows Only)

Handling the "Unknown Publisher" Warning

When you first run OptiSnap, Windows SmartScreen might pop up with a blue "Windows protected your PC" window. This is completely normal for new indie apps that haven't paid Microsoft for an expensive certificate yet.

[Screenshot: SmartScreen "Windows protected your PC"]
How to proceed:
  1. Click the hidden More Info link.
  2. Click the Run Anyway button that appears.

Mac Users

"App cannot be opened"

If macOS blocks the app, simply:

  1. Right-click the app icon.
  2. Select Open from the menu.
  3. Click Open in the dialog box.
Step 3

Activate License

Copy your license key from your email receipt (starts with SUMO-) and paste it into the ShopSnap startup screen.
